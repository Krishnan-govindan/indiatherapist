> "If you're happy to leave a review, it would help other NRIs find us.

**Example of a helpful review format (share as guidance, not a script):**
*"As an Indian in [city/country], I struggled to find a therapist who understood my situation. India Therapist connected me with a therapist who speaks [language] and understood [specific challenge] without needing background. Sessions were affordable and easy to schedule around [timezone]."*

Google uses review content for local search signals. Reviews mentioning "Indian therapist Sydney," "Hindi therapist online," "NRI therapy USA" directly boost search visibility for those terms.
